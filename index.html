About my all projects
My projects came to life through several apps that I used during their creation. Photoshop, XD, & Figma

Judaica Women Bags

Here I present an online shop of traditional handmade bags that speaks to the target audience of religious / traditional women, allowing for a convenient purchase experience with a reduction to the smallest details-viewing the fabric texture and the specific model's brief, among other things viewing and easily switching between the bag collection and the variety of models suitable to suit the religious woman's look.
The online store is available to computer and phone screens as an app.

Mariner watch shop app

In this project I present you an app for an existing luxury watch brand. Appealing to a target audience of water sports enthusiasts and providing its customers with the most aesthetic, clean and easy-to-use user experience, the animation is divided into three watch screens from different models. And it's meticulously detailed with a wide menu of commands that will get users to close a safe transaction.
