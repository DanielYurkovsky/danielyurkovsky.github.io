Mariner watch shop app

In this project I present you an app for an existing luxury watch brand. Appealing to a target audience of water sports enthusiasts and providing its customers with the most aesthetic, clean and easy-to-use user experience, the animation is divided into three watch screens from different models. And it's meticulously detailed with a wide menu of commands that will get users to close a safe transaction.

Re-Design Cadillac WebSite

View a custom-designed landing page to my taste and from the exact angle of my eye. The owner has adapted "a business card" for people who like the small details in their favorite brand. The page was designed interesting, appealing and juicy. The menu and navigation bar remain unchanged as on the brand's original website completely standardised. The changes came with lighthearted animation for the Cadillac brand's luxurious Escalade V model that added to its brutality.
